Flagship Projects

A selection of key projects from my airline analytics experience — covering dashboards, forecasting, ML, and automation.

★ Flagship Project
✈
Dashboard · Decision Platform
Captain — Flight-Level Performance Dashboard
Built a comprehensive flight-level snapshot system consolidating historical, current, and forward booking data into a single decision-making platform. Included competitor pricing, nonstop vs connection splits, and TY/LY comparisons — eliminating the need for multiple reports.
SQL Power BI Azure Databricks LF · Yield · RASM Competitor Pricing

Strategy · Pricing
Connect Report — Connection Market Optimization
Analyzed connection market performance by comparing filed vs. paid fares, demand by segment, and competitor impact. Combined past performance with forward booking trends to identify pricing inefficiencies and revenue gaps in multi-leg itineraries.
SQL Python Filed vs Paid Fares O&D Markets RASM/TRASM
📊
Forecasting · Diagnostics
Penguin — Revenue Performance & Forecasting
Built a revenue diagnostics and forecasting system analyzing LF, Yield, RPM, ASM, and RASM/TRASM together. Developed stage-linked metric adjustments to improve forecasting accuracy across markets with different route distances and capacities.
SQL Power BI RASM · TRASM Stage Adjustment Time Series
💼
Ancillary Revenue · Pricing
Bravo TPS — Ancillary Revenue Analytics
Developed a Power BI dashboard tracking take rates, sold factors, and pricing trends for bags, bundles, and premium seats. Integrated competitor pricing benchmarking and weekly performance monitoring to optimize non-ticket revenue streams.
Power BI SQL Take Rate Bundle Analytics Competitor Pricing
🚨
Automation · Anomaly Detection
Bravo Alerts — Automated Demand Anomaly System
Built an automated daily alerting system that detected unusual booking patterns in ancillary products using context-aware logic based on days-to-departure bands, market categories, and booking volume. Shifted the team from reactive to proactive decision-making.
SQL Automation DTD Bands Anomaly Detection
📈
KPI · Network Analytics
Performance Report — End-to-End Network Dashboard
Developed a full network performance dashboard with KPI panels, YoY market tables, quadrant analysis, ancillary revenue breakdown, fare class mix, and passenger segmentation — giving leadership complete visibility into pricing, demand, and capacity in one view.
Power BI SQL Quadrant Analysis Fare Class Mix YoY
💺
Product Analytics · Launch
Ala Carte — Premium Seat Product Launch Tracking
Tracked adoption, load factor, and take rates for a newly launched standalone premium seat product across daily, weekly, and monthly timeframes. Provided insights into customer willingness to pay independent of bundle purchases, validating a new standalone revenue strategy.
Power BI SQL Product Analytics Take Rate LF Tracking
🔮
Forecasting · Modeling
Demand & Revenue Forecasting Models
Developed demand forecasting using booking curve analysis and TY vs LY comparisons to predict Pax, LF, and RPM. Extended into revenue forecasting by integrating yield and pricing data to project RASM, TRASM, and total revenue — enabling proactive pricing decisions before departure.
Python SQL Azure Databricks Booking Curves RASM Forecast
